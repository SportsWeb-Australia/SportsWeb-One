About the Association
135 seasons of cricket in the east
The BHRDCA is an amateur 'hard wicket' cricket association centred around Melbourne's eastern suburbs, first established in 1890/91. We support Junior Boys & Girls, Senior, Women's and Veterans (Over 40 & Over 50) competitions — providing more than 3,500 cricketers of all ages and abilities the chance to play every week. New players, umpires and officials are always welcome.
