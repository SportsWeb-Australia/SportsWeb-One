Communications

How the BHRDCA keeps clubs, players, parents, officials and volunteers informed — the channels we use, what each one is for, and what we recommend you switch on so nothing important gets missed.

How we reach you
Different messages travel on different channels. Urgent, time-critical updates go by SMS and push; everything else by email, the website and social. Here's what each channel carries.
Recommended
Email & Mailing List
As needed · low volume
Official association and club communication — registration, season information, important notices, newsletters and event invitations.
Best for: things you'll want a record of.
Opt in: register with your club and join the BHRDCA mailing list.
Join the mailing list
Recommended
SMS / Text
Rare · urgent only
Time-critical alerts only — ground closures and washouts, last-minute fixture or venue changes, and team-selection messages from your club.
Best for: "you need to know this today" moments.
Opt in: keep a current mobile number with your club.
Register your mobile
Recommended
Push Notifications
Real-time · opt-in
Instant alerts from this website / app — new notices, results and breaking news, delivered to your phone or computer even when the site is closed.
Best for: the fastest heads-up, free and association-run.
Opt in: add this site to your home screen to enable alerts.
How to enable
Social Media
Regular · public
Community updates, match-day photos, results highlights, celebrations and the wider Victorian cricket conversation — on Facebook and Instagram.
Best for: the fun, the photos and the community feel.
Opt in: follow @bhrdca & @bhrdca1.
Follow the BHRDCA
Website & PlayHQ
Always on
Fixtures, results, ladders and live scores on PlayHQ, plus club information and news on this site — available any time, no sign-up. Add the site to your home screen for a one-tap app experience.
Best for: checking scores, draws and ladders on the go.
Find it: PlayHQ & this website.

News & Podcasts

Association news, match reports and the BHRDCA podcast — keeping the community across the talking points from around the grades all season long.
