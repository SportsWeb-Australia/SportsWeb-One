# The SportsWeb One admin app

This guide explains the SportsWeb One admin — the app you (and your committee)
use to run the club. It's separate from your club's public website: the website
is what supporters see; the admin is your back office.

## One front door for every club

`/admin` is the SportsWeb One entry page. It's **SportsWeb One branded** (not
club branded) on purpose — it's the same front door for every club on the
platform. You log in once and land in *your* club's admin, which then picks up
your club's colours and logo. Platform staff land on the platform console.

## Logging in

Two ways, whichever you prefer:

- **Email + password** — the usual way.
- **Magic link** — tap *"Email me a magic link instead"*. We email a one-tap
  link that signs you in securely with no password. Handy on a phone.

If you're signed in but see *"No club access"*, your login isn't linked to a
club yet — ask a club admin to add you.

## Install it like an app (PWA)

The admin is a Progressive Web App, so you can add it to your phone or computer
and it opens like a normal app — full screen, its own icon, no browser bar.

- **Android / Chrome / Edge (desktop):** an *"Install the SportsWeb One app"*
  button appears on the login screen — tap it.
- **iPhone / iPad (Safari):** tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home
  Screen**. The SportsWeb One icon appears on your home screen.

Once installed, opening it takes you straight to login, then your club pages.

## Finding your way around

The left menu groups everything. On a phone, tap the **menu (☰)** button at the
top to open it; picking anything closes it and drops you onto that screen.

Every major heading (Club Workspace, Modules, Club, Communications, Your
website, …) is a **collapsible group** — tap a heading to open it. They start
closed so the menu stays short and tidy.

## Security

Admin and committee accounts can secure their login with **two-factor
authentication (2FA)**. See `docs/security-mfa.md` for how that works (coming
with the security update).
